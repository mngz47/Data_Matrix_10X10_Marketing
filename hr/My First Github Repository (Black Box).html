My First Github Repository (Black Box)
Documentation

In the wake of a disheartening experience where all my hard work was pilfered and erased, I made a resolute decision to take control of my code and embrace the collaborative power of GitHub. It was time to turn the page and navigate the world of version control, learning that sometimes, joining the ranks of collaborators is the key to safeguarding one’s creations. As the saying goes, “If you can’t beat them, join them.” With this new resolve, my debut on GitHub takes the form of an ambitious project — an investing machine, a Black Box that holds the promise of transforming financial landscapes. In a world where finance is the beating heart of society, I saw an opportunity to contribute to this dynamic realm and make my mark. The initial setback with the mql5 currency pair system, utilizing the cross MA methodology, taught me invaluable lessons. While it may have faced challenges and setbacks after an initial burst of success, I’ve decided to make the code available for learning purposes. It’s a testament to the belief that shared knowledge fosters growth, even in the face of setbacks. Inspiration struck in the form of John Ghatti’s Nasdaq machine, a marvel that dances with the intricacies of harmonics and Fibonacci retracement in the unpredictable world of trading. His innovative approach captivated me, fueling the fire to create my own trading project. The allure of decoding market patterns and riding the waves of Fibonacci sequences became the driving force behind this new venture. As I embark on this journey into the fascinating realm of algorithmic trading, I find solace and excitement in the collaborative nature of GitHub. It’s not just about safeguarding my creations but about fostering a community where ideas flourish, and innovation thrives. So, here’s to a new chapter — the resilience after setbacks, the courage to share knowledge, and the inspiration drawn from pioneers like John Ghatti. As my Black Box project takes shape, it becomes not just a piece of code but a testament to the endless possibilities that unfold when like-minded individuals come together to unravel the mysteries of finance. The journey is just beginning, and I am eager to see where the collaborative spirit of GitHub will lead this endeavor.
